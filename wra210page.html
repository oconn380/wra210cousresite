    Design Philosophy

  1. I will make an effort to be inclusive of everyone and host a site that is both easily accessible and easily used by everyone. I believe that knowledge should be free to those who have the desire to learn, and that no one is more or less intelligent than another person but instead that intelligence appears in different forms. The information on my site should reflect this ideal in the sense that no one should have a hard time accessing or reading its content.
  2. My first goal is to learn how to create a website that follows the web standards. In order to achieve this goal my portfolio will need to fill out these web standards.
  3. My next goal is to Pass the class. I will need to work hard and do all of the assignments and try as hard as i can.
  4. My last goal is to Be prepared to take advanced web authoring in the future. I need to learn the topics covered in this class and master them.
